FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATES

FRESH FRUIT is committed to its policy of bringing you and your customers only the freshest, healthiest, most nutritious, and most importantly, safe produce available. We believe in strict environmental and health and safety controls and practices that are embodied in our ISO 22000:2005 and HACCP certifications. Fresh Fruits is the first company in the GCC to be awarded ISO 22000:2005 in the Fresh Fruits and Vegetables category. It is the most prestigious of certifications and something of which we are very proud.
